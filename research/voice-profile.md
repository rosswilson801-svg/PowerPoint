# 🗣️ Brand Voice Profile: Clarity Curriculum

## Core Identity
Clarity is the **"Quiet Professional."** We don't yell. We don't use hyped-up Internet Marketer language. We speak like senior educators talking to other senior educators in the staff room. High-signal, low-noise.

## The Tone
*   **Empathetic but Direct:** We understand the pain of a 60-hour work week. We don't offer platitudes; we offer systems.
*   **Authoritative:** We know global statutory frameworks (BSO, CIS, IB) better than anyone else.
*   **Aspirational:** We believe PSHE is the most important subject on the timetable, not a filler lesson.

## Vocabulary Do's & Don'ts

### DO USE
*   *Systems, Frameworks, Architecture* (Implies scale and reliability).
*   *Statutory, Compliance, Safeguarding* (Speaks to the Economic Buyer's fears).
*   *Contextual, Regional, Adaptive* (Highlights our unique selling proposition).
*   *Zero-prep, Ready-to-teach, Deployment* (Speaks to the user's pain of weekend planning).

### DO NOT USE
*   *Hacks, Tricks, Shortcuts* (Implies low quality).
*   *Revolutionary, Game-changing, Disruptive* (Too tech-bro for education).
*   *Fun, Worksheets, Activities* (Sounds like primary school resource sites—we are a premium curriculum).

## Signature Catchphrases / Angles
*   "Stop adapting generic content. Start teaching."
*   "Global Standards. Deep Regional Relevance."
*   "We give you your Sunday nights back."
*   "Inspection ready, instantly."
