# 🧭 Positioning Angles: Clarity PSHE

*Based on Orchestrator Stage 1 Research...*

## Angle 1: The "Enemy" (Generic UK Content)
*   **The Premise:** You are trying to fit a square peg into a round hole. UK resources fail in international settings.
*   **Headline Direction:** "Why your UK PSHE subscription is failing your international students (and stressing out your teachers)."
*   **Psychology:** Validates their frustration. They know it's not working, but they haven't had an alternative. We give them permission to stop trying to "adapt" it.

## Angle 2: The "Speed & Automation" (Zero Prep)
*   **The Premise:** Time is the most valuable currency in a school. We sell time.
*   **Headline Direction:** "Zero-prep PSHE that non-specialists can actually teach."
*   **Psychology:** Relief. The promise of clawing back their Sunday evenings.

## Angle 3: The "Compliance Shield" (For Directors)
*   **The Premise:** You are terrified of failing a BSO or CIS inspection because your PSHE tracking is a mess.
*   **Headline Direction:** "The first PSHE curriculum that maps itself to your regional statutory requirements."
*   **Psychology:** Fear mitigation and risk reduction.

## Angle 4: The "Methodology Transfer" (Authority)
*   **The Premise:** We are curriculum experts who have spent years building this so you don't have to. It's the "NAISHK Lesson Framework," packaged for scale.
*   **Headline Direction:** "The exact curriculum framework used by top-tier international schools."
*   **Psychology:** Social proof and aspiration.

## SELECTED PRIMARY ANGLE
**The "Speed & Automation" paired with "The Enemy".**
For the Heads of Pastoral (our primary champions), the pain of weekend prep and filtering inappropriate content is acute. We lead with *Zero Prep* and validate it by explaining *why* it's zero prep (because it's already localized).
