# 🔑 Keyword Strategy: Clarity PSHE

*Targeting the Information Gathering phase (Nov-April).*

## Core Objective
Capture "Solution-Aware" curriculum directors and "Problem-Aware" Heads of Pastoral when they are searching for ways to fix failing, non-compliant, or culturally inappropriate PSHE programs.

## 1. High Intent (Bottom of Funnel)
*These buyers have budget and are actively looking for a product.*
*   **"BSO compliant PSHE curriculum"** (High Value, Low Volume)
*   **"International school PSHE resources"** (High Value, Medium Volume)
*   **"Culturally adapted PSHE lesson plans"** (High Value, Low Volume)
*   **"CIS accredited wellbeing curriculum"** (High Value, Low Volume)

## 2. Problem Aware (Middle of Funnel)
*These buyers are currently experiencing pain and are looking for frameworks or fixes.*
*   **"How to teach relationships education in the Middle East"**
*   **"PSHE skills passport template"**

## 3. Top of Funnel (Educational / Awareness)
*Broad searches by teachers drowning in admin.*
*   **"Year 7 anxiety lesson plan"**

*   **"Best PSHE curriculum UK 2026"**

1.  **Pillar Post:** "The 2026 Guide to BSO-Compliant PSHE for International Schools" (Targeting the #1 compliance fear).
