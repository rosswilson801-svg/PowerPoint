# 🏛️ Structure: Content Pillars & Lead Magnets

*Building on the "Speed & Automation" and "Enemy" angles.*

## Pillar 1: The "Zero Prep" Educator
*   **Theme:** Reclaiming time, reducing stress, and eliminating the Sunday night scramble.
*   **Content Types:** Videos showing side-by-side time comparisons (DIY vs Clarity). Testimonials from teachers who now leave at 4 PM.
*   **Lead Magnet Link:** "Zero-Prep Regional Masterclass" (Slide Deck). The proof is in the pudding. Download it, teach it tomorrow.

## Pillar 2: The Cultural Minefield
*   **Theme:** Navigating international sensitivities without watering down the curriculum.
*   **Content Types:** Case studies: "How to teach Relationships in the Middle East." Checklists for vetting PSHE content for cultural friction.
*   **Lead Magnet Link:** "The International PSHE Audit Checklist." A tool for Curriculum Directors to assess their current provision's risk.

## Pillar 3: The Inspection Shield
*   **Theme:** Proving compliance and student wellbeing metrics to BSO, CIS, or local authorities.
*   **Content Types:** Walkthroughs of the Clarity Curriculum Planner dashboard. Explaining how we map to the English National Curriculum abroad.
*   **Lead Magnet Link:** "The BSO PSHE Evidence Guide." A mapping document showing exactly what inspectors look for.

## Current Lead Magnet Strategy
We execute on the **Pillar 1 Lead Magnet** first: The *Zero-Prep Regional Masterclass* slide deck. It serves the immediate pain of the end-user champion (the teacher/Head of Pastoral) and demonstrates instant value.
